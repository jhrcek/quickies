Elm LLM Quickies

Bunch of experimental / toy projects created with heavy LLM assistance

  • bayes
    Manipulable to explore Bayes' theorem
  • bezout
    Euclid's Algoritm and Bezout's identity visualization
  • binomial
    Binomial distribution calculator
  • click-searcher
    Search dictionary by clicking a word in text
  • congruence-equations
    Linear congruence equation solver
  • conics
    Interactive conic sections visualizer
  • covariance
    Covariance intuition builder
  • divisibility-lattice
    Interactive visualization of divisibility lattice
  • elm-cube
    3D cube visualization
  • eulers-totient
    Toy game for practicing steps to calculate Euler's totient function
  • hexagon
    Pascal's triangle in hexagonal grid
  • IEEE-754
    IEEE-754 floating point visualizer
  • sampling
    Random sampling toy
  • sampling-weighted
    Weighted random sampling toy
  • trigonometric-hyperbolic
    Mini reference of goniometric and trigonometric functions
  • water-pouring
    Water pouring puzzle solver
